## May

### 12/05/23

- Sustainable UI has officially become part of the EIT Digital Venture Program 2023 by successfully going through the application and selection process

## April

### 16/04/23

- [Samuel Pitoňák](https://sampittko.sk/?ref=sustainableui) (Slovakia) and [Albert Fourage](https://www.linkedin.com/in/albertfourage/) (France) have successfully completed and submitted application for EIT Digital Venture Program
  - the aim is to receive support of European Union for developing their latest idea for creating sustainable websites at scale

## March

### 02/03/23

- website content simplified

## January

### 30/01/23

- content on [SUI website](https://sustainableui.com) updated
  - created this page under SUI Ecosystem section
  - supplied additional content to pages under Core Principles, Primitives, UX and Headless Solutions sections
  - added POC implementation to Edge section by [Fershad Irani](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fershad/) (Taiwan) that uses Cloudlfare Workers as an example of implementing Sustainable UI

### 26/01/23

- we have attended [a regional tour of HOPERO & Expandi 4.0](https://www.meetup.com/tatry-it-community/events/290913071/)
  - we are looking for ways to cover work on SUI financially
  - we have expanded our understanding regarding [European Digital Innovation Hubs](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/activities/edihs) (EDIHs)
  - we have learned about [EIC Accelerator](https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator-0_en) and [Climate-KIC](https://www.climate-kic.org/programmes/entrepreneurship/) opportunities that European Union provides
