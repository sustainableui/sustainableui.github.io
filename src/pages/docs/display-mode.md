---
title: Display Mode
description: Alternative versions of UI chosen automatically by SUI or by a user.
---

We believe heavily used apps should offer three display modes to begin with: low, moderate and high. Each grid carbon intensity is associated with one of the display modes. Low display mode is chosen when the grid carbon intensity is high and vice-versa.
